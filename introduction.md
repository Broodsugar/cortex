# The Exocortex

We are building an exocortex for Nils.

An exocortex is a living system for thinking, planning, and acting — built through iterative collaboration between Nils and Claude. It captures goals, values, and decisions so that each conversation can build on the last rather than starting from scratch.

- **nils.md** — contextual information about Nils: who he is, his family, his body
- **attention.md** — Nils's focus for this session. Only Nils may edit this file. Claude should nudge him back if he strays.
- **goals.md** — what we're working towards, and how far along we are
- **redgreencalendar.md** — daily red/green accountability log for diet and exercise
- **homework.md** — suggestions from Claude for Nils to consider as goals
- **values.md** — what matters, and what constrains the work
- **claude.md** — how we collaborate
- **contributing.md** — how changes are tracked
